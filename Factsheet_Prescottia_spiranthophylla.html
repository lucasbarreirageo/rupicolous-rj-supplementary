Justification

Prescottia spiranthophylla is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 3,544.4 km²; AOO = 12 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 2 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (2) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.1 Annual & perennial non-timber crops; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL BARRA DE SANTANA | RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL NÃO PENSEI.
