Justification

Barbacenia squamata is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 1,461.5 km²; AOO = 12 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 3 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (3) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): APA DA ORLA MARÍTIMA | PARQUE NACIONAL DA TIJUCA | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL SÃO DOMINGOS | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DE TRÊS PICOS | PARQUE ESTADUAL DOS TRÊS PICOS.
