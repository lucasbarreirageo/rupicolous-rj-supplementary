Justification

Begonia tripicoensis is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 4 km² (Raw EOO = 0 km² was normalized to AOO according to §4.10 RLG); AOO = 4 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 1 location determined by the threat. Assessment: CR under criterion B1 (EOO < 100 km²). Criterion: B1ab(iv)+B2ab(iv). The number of locations (1) meets the IUCN threshold for CR (≤ 1). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 10.3 Avalanches/landslides. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DE TRÊS PICOS | PARQUE ESTADUAL DOS TRÊS PICOS | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DA BACIA DO RIO MACACU.
