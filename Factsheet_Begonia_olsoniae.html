Justification

Begonia olsoniae is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 16,076.6 km²; AOO = 48 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 9 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: VU under criterion B1 (EOO < 20,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (9) meets the IUCN threshold for VU (≤ 10). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.2 Wood & pulp plantations; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching; 10.3 Avalanches/landslides.
