Justification

Quesnelia lateralis is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 3,596.7 km²; AOO = 72 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 14 locations determined by the threat. Category: LC/NT — the quantitative thresholds for IUCN Criteria B and D were not met.
