Justification

Pitcairnia encholirioides is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 12 km² (Raw EOO = 1.8 km² was normalized to AOO according to §4.10 RLG); AOO = 12 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 3 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (3) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL SÃO GERÔNIMO | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL SÃO DOMINGOS.
