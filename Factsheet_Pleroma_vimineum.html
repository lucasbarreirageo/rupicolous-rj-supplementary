Justification

Pleroma vimineum is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 12,525.6 km²; AOO = 80 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 14 locations determined by the threat. Category: LC/NT — the quantitative thresholds for IUCN Criteria B and D were not met.
