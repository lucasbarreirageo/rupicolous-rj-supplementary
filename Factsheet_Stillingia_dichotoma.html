Justification

Stillingia dichotoma is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 2,173.6 km²; AOO = 16 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 4 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (4) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): MONUMENTO NATURAL MUNICIPAL DA PEDRA DE ITAOCAIA | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL MUNICIPAL DAS SERRAS DE MARICÁ | PARQUE ESTADUAL DA SERRA DA TIRIRICA | PARQUE ESTADUAL DO DESENGANO | REFÚGIO DE VIDA SILVESTRE SERRA DA CACUNDA.
