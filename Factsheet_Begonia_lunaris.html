Justification

Begonia lunaris is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 196.5 km²; AOO = 28 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 4 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (4) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL RESERVA ECOLÓGICA DE GUAPIAÇU | RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL REGUA IV | PARQUE ESTADUAL DOS TRÊS PICOS | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DA BACIA DO RIO MACACU | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DA REGIÃO SERRANA DE PETRÓPOLIS.
