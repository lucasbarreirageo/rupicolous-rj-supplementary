Justification

Barbacenia brevifolia is endemic to the state of Rio de Janeiro, with a rupicolous habit. The spatial analysis of confirmed occurrences (EOO = 554 km²; AOO = 20 km², 2×2 km grid) identified 5 locations determined by the threat. Assessment: EN under criterion B1 (EOO < 5,000 km²). Criterion: B1ab(i,ii,iii,iv)+B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv). The number of locations (5) meets the IUCN threshold for EN (≤ 5). Active threats (IUCN-CMP v3.3): 1.1 Housing & urban areas; 2.2 Wood & pulp plantations; 2.3 Livestock farming & ranching. Occurs in Protected Areas (PA regime: Integral Protection and Sustainable Use): RESERVA PARTICULAR DO PATRIMÔNIO NATURAL GRAZIELA MACIEL BARROSO | MONUMENTO NATURAL ESTADUAL DA SERRA DA MARIA COMPRIDA | RESERVA BIOLÓGICA DE ARARAS | ÁREA DE PROTEÇÃO AMBIENTAL DA REGIÃO SERRANA DE PETRÓPOLIS.
